Support

Most problems come from photo quality, network hiccups, or billing. Start here before emailing.

Common issues

My reading says the lines are unclear

The model needs four things: a flat palm, natural light (not flash), no rings or bandages, and the whole palm in frame. Shoot near a window, not under a lamp.

The app crashed or froze

Force quit (swipe up) and reopen. If it keeps happening, restart your phone. Crashes are auto-reported to us via Sentry, so you do not need to email a log.

I was charged but do not see Premium

Open the app, go to Settings → Restore Purchases. If that fails, the receipt is held by Apple — check Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions on your phone.

How do I cancel?

iOS handles this: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → Palm Reader → Cancel. We cannot cancel on your behalf.

Delete my reading history

Settings → Clear History inside the app. History lives only on your device — we have no server copy to delete.

Still stuck?

Email dev@moruk.ai with your iOS version and a short description. Attach a screenshot if you can. We reply within a few days.
