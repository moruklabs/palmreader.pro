Cookie Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

The short version

Palm Reader is a native iOS app. The app itself does not use HTTP cookies. This website (palmreader.pro) is static marketing content and does not set tracking cookies either.

What the app stores locally

On your iPhone, the app uses standard iOS storage for your reading history and subscription status. That is not a cookie — it is on-device preferences, managed by iOS and cleared when you delete the app.

What the website stores

Nothing. No analytics cookies, no ad cookies, no consent banner required. If you load an embedded App Store badge, Apple may set its own cookies under their policy.
