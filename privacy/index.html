Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 23, 2026

What we collect

When you use Palm Reader, we receive the palm photo you upload and a small amount of device metadata (iOS version, app version, crash logs). We do not ask for your name, email, or location.

How photos are handled

Your palm photo is sent to our vision model, a reading is generated, and the photo is discarded within seconds. We do not keep copies. We do not train models on your photos.

What we store on your device

Your reading history is stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage. You can delete it at any time from Settings → Clear History. Nothing in your history is uploaded to us.

Third parties

  • Apple (App Store analytics — anonymous)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)
  • Sentry (crash reports — no personal data)

Your rights

Because we do not retain personal data tied to your identity, there is usually nothing for us to export or delete on request. If you want to raise a concern anyway, email dev@moruk.ai.

Changes

We will post any material changes to this page and update the date above.
