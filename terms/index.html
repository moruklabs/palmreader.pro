What Palm Reader is

Palm Reader is an entertainment app. It generates a reading based on the photo you upload and a palmistry knowledge base. It is not fortune telling, it is not medicine, and it is not life advice. Treat readings the way you would treat a horoscope in a newspaper.

What Palm Reader is not

  • A medical tool. Do not skip a doctor because of a reading.
  • A financial advisor. Do not make money decisions based on a reading.
  • A legal oracle. We are not responsible for choices you make after reading one.
